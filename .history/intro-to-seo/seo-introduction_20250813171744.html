What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, a set of practices to improve how your site appears in search engine organic results.

In other words, SEO helps people find your website without paying for ads.

Next

Why SEO Matters

Good SEO increases your website's visibility and helps people find you organically.

More visibility means more visitors without spending on ads.
