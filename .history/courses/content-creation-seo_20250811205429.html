Structure for Readability

Use headings, bullet points, and short paragraphs to make text scannable.

A reader-friendly layout not only helps visitors but also signals quality to search engines.

Next

Expertise and trust

Keep your content fresh and accurate.

Fresh content can improve rankings and user satisfaction.
