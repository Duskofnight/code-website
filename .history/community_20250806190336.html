How do you stay focused while coding?

Do you guys have any tips to staying focused while coding? I always tend to just lose track of where I am or get distracted by my phone. It's really affecting my productivity and I'm starting to feel frustrated about it.

Alex Phan 1 hour ago

Try putting your phone in another room or using apps that block distracting websites during coding sessions. I use Focus and it's been a game changer for my productivity.
